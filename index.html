You're not alone

Making medical decisions can feel overwhelming. Here are some resources that may help.

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
    Call or text 988 — available 24/7
  • Crisis Text Line
    Text HOME to 741741
  • SAMHSA Helpline
    1-800-662-4357 — mental health & substance use

This tool is not a substitute for professional support. Please speak with your care team or a counselor if you are struggling.
